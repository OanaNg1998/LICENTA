Who are we?

We are a group of young fitness instructors passionate about IT, who built this application to help their clients.

Who are our clients?

A big part of the people we train are those who spend a lot of time in the chair, at the office or who travel a lot in the interest of work.
Just Move was built to solve the problem of excuses that customers have when it comes to sports.

Who are our sponsors?

The gyms presented on the site are old collaborators, each gym having a very well trained staff able to meet the needs of
the most demanding customers.
